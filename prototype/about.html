About ElevAIte

A calmer calendar, made by people who hated theirs.

We're a small team in Palo Alto building the tool we wished existed when we were students juggling classes, side projects, and group work.

We started ElevAIte because every calendar we tried felt like a chore.

Cron got close. Notion Calendar (after the acquisition) kept the soul. But neither was built for students drowning in recurring class schedules, or tiny startup teams who can't justify $12/seat Calendly bills just to share a link.

So we made the version we wished existed: warm cream, coral accents, Geist Mono in the gutter, no spinners, no dark patterns. Free forever if you're a student. Free forever for personal use. We make money from teams who want round-robin and SSO — that's it.

If you want a calendar that doesn't make you flinch when you open it, we built this for you.

What we believe.

A handful of opinions we hold loudly.

01

Calmer is better.

No notification fatigue. No urgent badges. No animations that demand your attention.

02

Warm beats cold.

Cream beats pure white. Coral beats Slack-blue. Geist Mono beats system-ui.

03

Free for people, paid for teams.

Individuals never pay. Students never pay. We make money where it actually creates value: teams.

04

Your data is yours.

We don't store your events. We hold OAuth tokens. You can export and delete anytime.

05

Ship every two weeks.

No surprise launches. No big bang releases. Just steady, small, visible improvements.

06

Never train AI on your stuff.

Not on your events. Not on your messages. Not on anything that's yours.
