TL;DR

We don't store your calendar event data on our servers. We hold OAuth tokens and account preferences only. We never sell your data, never serve ads, and never train AI on your events. You can export everything or delete your account at any time.

What we collect

When you create an ElevAIte account, we collect:

  • Your name, email, and (optionally) profile picture from your OAuth provider
  • OAuth tokens for your connected calendar accounts (Google, Apple, Microsoft) — these grant us read/write access
  • Preferences you set (time zones, view defaults, keyboard shortcuts, theme)
  • Anonymous diagnostic data (crash reports, performance metrics) — opt-out available in Settings → Privacy

How we use what we collect

We use the data we collect for three things only:

  • Operating the product: showing your calendar, syncing changes, sending reminders.
  • Supporting you: if you email hello@elevaite.so, we may look at your account to help.
  • Improving the product: aggregated, anonymous usage metrics — never tied to your identity.

Calendar data — the important part

We do not store the contents of your calendar events on our servers. Event titles, attendees, descriptions, and locations live with your original provider (Google, Apple, Microsoft) and are fetched on demand each time you open the app.

What this means in practice:

  • We can't read your events when you're not signed in.
  • If you disconnect your calendar account, we immediately lose all access.
  • Your event data is never used to train AI or machine learning models.
  • Scheduling links (when invitees pick a time) cache only the slot you're booking — never your private events.

Who we share data with

We share data with:

  • Your calendar providers (Google, Apple, Microsoft) — to read and write events on your behalf.
  • Stripe — to process Team and Enterprise subscriptions. We never see your full card number.
  • Linear, Slack, Notion — only if you explicitly connect those integrations.

We do not sell your data. We do not share it with advertisers. We never have.

Cookies + tracking

We use minimal first-party cookies for signed-in sessions and to remember your preferences. We don't use third-party advertising cookies, fingerprinting, or session replay. Our analytics are server-side, aggregated, and don't identify you.

Your rights (GDPR, CCPA, FERPA)

You can, at any time:

  • Export all your data (Settings → Account → Export full data)
  • Delete your account permanently within 24 hours
  • Disconnect any calendar account immediately — we lose access in real time
  • Opt out of diagnostic data collection (Settings → Privacy)
  • For .edu users: we comply with FERPA. Class schedule data is treated as student educational record data.

Storage + security

Account preferences and OAuth tokens are stored in AWS us-west-2 with encryption at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3+). Enterprise customers can request data residency in EU or APAC. We are SOC 2 Type II audited; the report is available under NDA.

Children

ElevAIte is not directed at children under 13. The .edu Student plan is intended for university students. If you become aware of a child under 13 using the service, please email us at privacy@elevaite.so.
