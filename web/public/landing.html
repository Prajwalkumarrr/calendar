FAQ

Questions, answered.

How is ElevAIte different from Notion Calendar?
Same keyboard-first philosophy and event design. We're built for students and startup teams specifically — that means recurring class schedules, .edu free tier, team availability, and unlimited scheduling links built in. No $12/seat Calendly replacement needed.
Is my calendar data stored on your servers?
No. Event data stays on your devices and the original provider (Google, iCloud, Outlook). We hold OAuth tokens and your preferences — nothing else. You can revoke access at any time.
Which calendar providers do you support?
Google Calendar, iCloud Calendar, Microsoft Outlook (personal + work). CalDAV support is in beta. You can connect as many accounts as you want.
What counts as a “student” for the free tier?
Any active student with a .edu (or international equivalent) email address. We verify by sending a confirmation email. Renews automatically each term while your address stays active.
Is there a mobile app?
Yes — native iOS app on the App Store, and a polished mobile web experience for Android. A native Android app is on the roadmap for Q3.
Can I import my data if I leave?
Always. We never trap you. Settings → About → Export full data. Your events stay in their source calendars regardless — we’re just a beautiful viewer.
