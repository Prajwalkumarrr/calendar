Pricing FAQs

Is the Personal plan really free forever?
Yes, no asterisks. We make money from teams. We never plan to add ads, sell your data, or downgrade Personal features.
What if my .edu expires (graduation)?
We give you a 6-month grace period after your .edu stops verifying, then you’re automatically moved to Personal (which is still free). Your data and scheduling links stay intact.
Can I switch plans later?
Upgrade or downgrade anytime. Team is pro-rated daily. No annual lock-in.
Do you offer refunds?
30-day money-back for Team and Enterprise. Just email billing@elevaite.so — no forms, no friction.
