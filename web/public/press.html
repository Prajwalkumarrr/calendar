The boilerplate

Drop-in description, ~50 words.

ElevAIte is a warm, keyboard-first calendar built for students and startup teams. Founded in 2024 in Palo Alto, the company is backed by Y Combinator (W26), Neo, AI Grant, and South Park Commons. ElevAIte is free for personal use, free forever for students with a .edu, and $6/seat for teams.
